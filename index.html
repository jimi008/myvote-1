WHERE DOES YOUR VOTE STAND

MY Vote SPORT is a social voting game where you get to have your say on the biggest sporting debates. It's about where your vote stands amongst the public - are you in or out of the crowd.

THE CROWD COINSTM SCORING SYSTEM

Every time you guess correctly whether you're in the majority or the minority of voters, you're in the crowd or out of the crowd, you win 2 Crowd Coins. If you get it wrong, you lose 1 Coin.

WHO KNOWS THE CROWD SENSE

Find out who understands the crowd's mood and who doesn't by setting up a league and playing against your friends.

EARN REWARDS

Make your way up the ranks by winning badges for your profile. As you earn Crowd CoinsTM, you can exchange them for sponsored products and rewards.
